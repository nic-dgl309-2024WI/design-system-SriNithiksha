Cards


Cards are surfaces where actions and content are displayed related to a single subject. Finding pertinent and useful information should be simple when scanning them. It is important to arrange elements on them in a way that makes sense of hierarchy, such as text and photos.

Cards Example

Joey Witman
"Choosing [Your Company Name] was one of the best decisions I've made. Their expertise and dedication helped me achieve remarkable results. I couldn't be happier."
