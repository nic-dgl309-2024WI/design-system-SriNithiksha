Carousel


A carousel in web design is an interactive element that showcases multiple pieces of content, like images, videos, or text, in a rotating manner. It typically features navigation controls, allowing users to move between content items manually or automatically. Carousels are commonly used on websites to highlight featured content, such as products, services, or promotions, in an engaging and visually appealing way.
